About Us

Welcome to CozyCup Café: A Story Brewed with Passion CozyCup Café isn't just a coffee shop; it's a dream realized, a testament to the unwavering dedication and shared vision of its founders, Mark Rodney Galleon and Robin Vail Bacosa. This journey began not with a business plan, but with a shared love for the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee, the comforting warmth of a cozy space, and the simple joy of connecting with people. Mark, a lifelong coffee enthusiast with a keen eye for detail and a passion for crafting the perfect cup, has spent years honing his skills as a barista. His experience instilled in him a deep appreciation for the art of coffee making—from sourcing the finest beans to mastering the delicate balance of flavors. His expertise ensures that every cup served at CozyCup is a testament to quality and craftsmanship. Robin, with her sharp business acumen and a natural talent for creating welcoming environments, brings her background to the café's operations. She's the driving force behind CozyCup's inviting atmosphere, ensuring that every detail, from the décor to the friendly service, contributes to a truly memorable experience for each guest. Together, Mark and Robin envisioned a space where the community could gather, relax, and connect over a cup of exceptional coffee. They sought to create more than just a café; they wanted to build a haven, a place where the everyday stresses of life could melt away, replaced by the comforting warmth of friendship and the invigorating energy of a perfectly brewed cup. Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We source our beans directly from sustainable farms, ensuring ethical practices and the highest quality ingredients. We believe in supporting local businesses and farmers, and our commitment to sustainability extends beyond our beans. More than just coffee, CozyCup Café is about creating a space where memories are made, connections are forged, and the simple pleasures of life are savored. We invite you to become a part of our CozyCup family. Come, relax, connect, and discover your perfect brew. We look forward to welcoming you!
